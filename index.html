So What is an Archaeologist?

What comes to mind when you picture an Archaeologist?

Someone with a trowel and brush, wearing khaki shorts and a hat, digging industriously in some hot, dry area for… dinosaurs?

Umm, no. Here be no dinosaurs! But, I promise, even without the bones of behemoths, Archaeology is pretty cool!

Take a look around my website to see some of the things that Archaeologists do.

What's with the Website?

This website was created for ANTH 485 – Digital Archaeology!

In this class we are learning some of the wonderful benefits (and interesting drawbacks) of using digital technology to present archaeological finds and data.

This website is a space for me to showcase different projects that I work on for this class as well as a space to work out some of my thoughts about the accessibility and sustainability of Digital Archaeology and my feelings about the field that is so dear to me
